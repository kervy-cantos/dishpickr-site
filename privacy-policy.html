DishPickr Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

DishPickr is a meal planning and grocery list app that helps users pick dishes, plan meals, save recipes, and create grocery lists.

Information We Collect

DishPickr does not require users to create an account. We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or login credentials.

The app may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Custom dishes or recipes you create
  • Meal plans you add
  • Grocery list items
  • Selected dish images
  • App preferences such as onboarding and premium status

Local Storage

DishPickr stores your meal plans, recipes, grocery items, and app preferences locally on your device. This data is not uploaded to our servers because DishPickr currently does not use an account system or cloud sync.

Images

DishPickr may allow you to select images from your device for custom dishes or recipes. These images are used only inside the app to display your custom dishes. DishPickr does not upload these images to our servers.

Advertising

DishPickr may show advertisements using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain device information to provide, measure, and improve advertising. This may include identifiers, approximate location, device information, and ad interaction data depending on your device settings and Google’s policies.

You can learn more about how Google uses information from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Premium Features and Purchases

DishPickr may offer premium features such as removing ads, unlocking unlimited custom dishes, and enabling additional grocery planning tools. Payments and purchases are handled by the app store or payment provider. DishPickr does not directly collect or store your payment card information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. DishPickr may use third-party services such as Google AdMob for advertising. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

DishPickr is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Deletion

Since DishPickr stores recipe, meal plan, and grocery data locally on your device, you can delete this data by clearing app data, uninstalling the app, or using any available reset options inside the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
